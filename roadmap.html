Roadmap

Clear, staged rollout from core product to a scaled, global challenge-to-earn ecosystem.

Q1 2026 — Foundation

Finalize AI verification stack (face, motion and object checks), stabilize the core challenge engine and run closed pilots with early users and selected brands in 1–2 key markets.

Q2 2026 · TOKEN LAUNCH

Q2 2026 — Token Launch & Growth Engine

CHLG token launch, on-chain rewards going live, basic staking for higher-tier tasks and Creator Mode v1 so creators can propose and run their own challenges on top of the platform.

Q3 2026 — Ecosystem & Partnerships

FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions from users and investors.

What is CHLG? CHLG is a challenge-to-earn crypto app where users complete real-life challenges, upload short-form content (photo or video), pass AI verification, and earn CHLG tokens as a reward.
How do I earn CHLG tokens? Do a challenge, post your proof on the app, and get rewarded once AI verifies it. You can also join from TikTok or Instagram by posting with the #CHLG hashtag.
Is the app free to use? Yes. Browsing challenges and submitting content is free. In the future there may be premium features for creators, brands, and power-users, but the core challenge-to-earn loop is designed to stay accessible.
How does AI verification work? We use a multi-layer AI pipeline that checks face consistency, body movement, camera motion, frame-by-frame artifacts, and metadata to detect deepfakes, re-uploads, or scripted cheating. Suspicious submissions are flagged or rejected before rewards are distributed.
What stops people from cheating with old or stolen videos? CHLG combines time-limited prompts, randomization, and motion-specific actions. If content looks reused, edited, or inconsistent, the submission fails verification.
Where are my tokens stored? Tokens appear first in your in-app balance. Later, you can withdraw them to a personal wallet, swap them, or use them in the ecosystem.
On which blockchain will CHLG launch? CHLG will launch on a gas-efficient, EVM-compatible chain. The final network will be announced closer to token generation.
Is my data and content safe? We analyze only what’s required for verification and security. A transparent, globally compliant privacy policy will be published at launch.
Can brands and creators sponsor their own challenges? Yes. “Challenge Studio” will allow brands, creators, and communities to launch paid or promotional challenges powered by CHLG rewards.
What stage is CHLG in right now? CHLG is preparing the mobile app, ecosystem, tokenomics, and partnerships. This one-pager is for early collaborators and investors.
